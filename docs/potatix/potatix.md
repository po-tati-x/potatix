## Potatix summary

My company Potatix, is developing [a defined offering] to help [defined audience] [solve a problem] with [secret sauce]

(notes: Example: My company, the Founder Institute, is developing a startup accelerator to help idea-stage and pre-seed entrepreneurs build fundable businesses with a structured methodology and global investor network.
Don't use adjectives, particularly superlatives. Never say 'first', 'only', 'huge' or 'best' as these words signal inexperience
Do properly define your target market. For example, 'women' or 'small businesses' are way too large and not nearly targeted enough
Don't use any buzzwords, acronyms or industry jargon in your pitch
Do keep it short. It's easy to write a long sentence, but the right thing is to be concise)
